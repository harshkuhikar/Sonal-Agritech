Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy and for your protection. We will only use the information that we collect about you lawfully (in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998).

Customer information is collected to process your order and to provide you with the best possible service. Information is collected as a result of you contacting us, placing an order, or requesting registration. We store lists of email addresses, which we use to send out information about our products and services.

You will receive information e-mails from us only if you give your consent. If you do not wish to receive such emails, you can remove yourself by clicking on the link at the bottom of an information e-mail. You may also remove yourself by visiting the ‘my tree’ members section of Sonal Agritech and completing the ‘remove from mailing list’ section.

We will not collect sensitive information about you without your consent. The information we hold will be accurate and up to date. We may use technology to track the patterns of behaviour of visitors to our site. This can include using a ‘cookie’ which would be stored on your browser. The information collected in this way can be used to identify you unless you modify your browser settings. If you have any questions/comments about privacy, please email us at info@sonalagritech.co.in.
