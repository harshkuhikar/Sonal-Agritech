A trusted nursery partner plays a crucial role in bringing every landscape to life - providing quality, consistency, and plant diversity at scale. At Sonal-Agritech, our wholesale nursery service ensures timely availability of healthy, climate-suitable plants for projects of all sizes.

Whether you're a developer, government body, resort planner, or landscape architect, our nursery operations deliver not just volume, but value, variety, and vitality.

Landscaping Styles for Commercial Spaces

  • Large-Scale Capacity: Spread across acres of fertile growing zones, ready to meet bulk project needs.

  • Climate-Matched Varieties: From drought-tolerant natives to tropical exotics, every plant is suited for regional success.

  • Quality Assurance: Regular health checks, soil conditioning, pest screening, and pre-dispatch inspections.

  • Rooted in Sustainability: Organically nurtured, pesticide-safe, and environment-first practices.

  • Bulk Logistics Support: On-site tagging, bundling, and scheduled delivery with project coordination.
